What is the Mega Placement Drive?

The Mega Placement Drive is an exclusive event to connect talented candidates with top companies, offering exciting career opportunities.

Who can participate in the event?

Final-year students, fresh graduates, and candidates looking for job opportunities across various industries can participate.

How do I register for the drive?

Visit the registration section on our landing page, fill out the form, and upload your resume to secure your spot.

What companies will participate in the event?

Leading companies across IT, finance, healthcare, and other sectors will participate. The list of participating companies will be updated soon.

Are there any fees to participate?

No, participation in the Mega Placement Drive is completely free for candidates.

How will I know my schedule for the event?

Once registered, you will receive an email with your event schedule and further details.
